Point CMake at the source dir where you decompressed VIPRE.

Help Cmake find your install of dcmtk, boost and OSG.
Qt is optional.

Minimum required versions:
Boost 1.47 or greater
Dcmtk 3.6.0 or greater
OSG 3.0.1 or greater
Qt 4.7.4 or greater

Configure, generate your build files.

Build VIPRE.

For more specific instructions on how to build each dependency library on
a specific OS, please refer to the VIPRE wiki on Github.
